The Sovereign Vision

Why Hecos was built — and what makes it different.

The Mission & The Creator

Hecos Core was born from a simple but powerful idea: Digital Sovereignty. In a world where AI is increasingly centralized and cloud-monitored, Hecos offers a private, local alternative where the user is the only master of their data.

Created by Antonio Meloni (Tony), Hecos is a labor of love for the Sovereign AI community. It is designed for those who believe that intelligence should live on the user's hardware, not on a remote server. Hecos is more than an agent; it is a native operating layer that bridges high-level reasoning with root-level system execution.

Why Hecos?

Built different. Designed to be your sovereign AI operating layer.

🧠

Operating Layer vs Agent

Hecos isn't just an agent; it's the Operating Layer. It manages resources, drivers, and cognitive profiles, allowing multiple agents to coexist and operate in a unified system.

🧩

Modular Tiering

Architecture divided into Core Modules (Stability), Plugins (Logic), and Extensions (System Tools). Fully customizable for any mission.

🌐

MCP Native Support

Integrated Model Context Protocol (MCP) bridge. Connect seamlessly to standardized servers for infinite data and tool orchestration.

🔀

Local & Cloud Fluidity

Designed for absolute local privacy, but ready for the cloud: works entirely offline with Ollama or KoboldCpp, or scales to extreme performance using any major cloud provider.
