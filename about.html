The Mission & The Creator

Adapting Technology for Humanity

Hecos Core was born from a pragmatic and fundamental objective: to improve human life. We are not interested in the abstract concept of 'digital sovereignty', a term devoid of logical sense in a technological landscape destined to evolve radically into forms that are unpredictable today. Digital is just a current tool, not the end.

Hecos embraces a holistic vision of technology. We recognize that both digital and **analog** have their unique advantages; analog should not be discarded a priori as many believe, but integrated where it offers better solutions. Whether technology is digital, analog, or something more evolved that is yet to come, what matters only is its capacity to serve humanity.

Hecos is Local-First. Your entire knowledge base — emails, contacts, and RAG databases — strictly resides on your machine. If you choose to use a Cloud model for more power, Hecos only sends the specific prompt via secure API channels, preventing your sensitive business data from being used to train Big Tech models.

Created by Antonio Meloni (Tony), Hecos is designed for those who believe that intelligence must serve the user on their hardware. Hecos is more than an agent; it is a native operating layer that unites high-level reasoning with root-level system execution. We reject the intrinsically wrong idea of 'Sovereign AI': AI must not be sovereign, it must be functional. For this reason, Hecos integrates **direct commands** to automate every operation, ensuring efficiency even without AI intervention.
